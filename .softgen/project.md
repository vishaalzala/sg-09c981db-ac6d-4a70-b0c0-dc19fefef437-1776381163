# Automotive Workshop Management SaaS

## Vision
Production-ready multi-tenant SaaS platform for automotive mechanical workshops in New Zealand/Australia. Complete workshop operating system with CRM, operations, compliance, and customer-facing features.

**Target users:** Workshop owners, service advisors, technicians, WOF inspectors, customers

## Design
- **Primary:** 220 90% 28% (deep navy blue - automotive professional)
- **Accent:** 35 100% 50% (amber/orange - workshop safety)
- **Secondary:** 210 25% 45% (steel blue)
- **Muted:** 220 15% 96% (light blue-grey)
- **Destructive:** 0 70% 50% (red)
- **Success:** 145 65% 45% (green)
- **Warning:** 45 95% 55% (yellow)
- **Background:** 0 0% 100% (white)
- **Foreground:** 220 25% 15% (dark slate)

**Fonts:** Plus Jakarta Sans (headings, 600/700), Work Sans (body, 400/500)

**Style:** Dense professional layouts, status-driven colors, tab navigation, data tables with clear hierarchy

## Features
**Core Operations:** Multi-tenant SaaS with customer/vehicle CRM, bookings calendar, job management, quotes, invoices, payments, supplier/procurement, inventory management

**Add-ons (paid):** WOF compliance system, CARJAM integration (usage billing), marketing campaigns, website builder, loyalty program

**User Roles:** Super Admin, Company Owner, Branch Manager, Service Advisor, Technician, Parts Manager, WOF Inspector, Accountant, Reception, Customer Portal User

**Key Workflows:** Global search (customer/vehicle/rego/VIN), inline customer/vehicle creation, customer merge, vehicle move, reminder engine, smart check-in tablet mode, customer portal
